ANPR features

Country independent

Each country has its laws regarding number plates. Our ANPR system is customizable to suit each of them.

Adaptable to lights

The product adapts itself to the lighting conditions with its decision making features. Also, it can adjust with reflections. Thereby, it extracts the exact alphanumerics.

High-speed processing

The product identifies the characters even if the vehicle is at high speed. Never miss any data with ANPR.

Adjusts to Plate inconsistency

Each number plate differs in format, colour, font, layout, size. Our ANPR adjusts with these to get accurate data.

Fix Recognition issue

Detect characters even in obstructive conditions like skew angles, dirt, and objects like screws and adhesives. Enhance recognition of your surveillance system.

Flag images for attention

When number plate conditions are worse, which makes characters unreadable, the system flags images. A supervisor can attend to it and recognize the same.

Mass identification

The product takes non-stop live feed from your surveillance systems. It analyzes large amounts of data instantly in real-time.
